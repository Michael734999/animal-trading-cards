Lion

A lion's roar can be heard up to eight kilometres away.

  • Average Lifespan: 14 years
  • Average Weight: 160kg
  • Max Speed: 80km/h
  • Diet: Carnivore

Lions have strong, compact bodies and powerful forelegs, teeth and jaws for pulling down and killing prey.

Kangaroo

Some Kangaroos Can Hop 25 Feet.

  • Average Lifespan: 6 years
  • Average Weight: 47kg
  • Max Speed: 70km/h
  • Diet: Herbivore

The kangaroo is a marsupial with large, powerful hind legs, large feet adapted for leaping, a long muscular tail for balance, and a small head.

Elephant

Elephants are the world's largest land animal!

  • Average Lifespan: 60 years
  • Average Weight: 5000kg
  • Max Speed: 40km/h
  • Diet: Herbivore

Elephant, characterized by its long trunk, columnar legs, and huge head with temporal glands and wide, flat ears.
